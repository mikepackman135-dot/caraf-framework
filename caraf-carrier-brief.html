The Documentation Gap

The Cosigner Problem

The medical record can prove the AI spoke. EHR metadata, audit logs, and API call records establish that a tool generated output during a patient encounter. What they cannot prove is whether the physician engaged — read it, questioned it, applied independent judgment, or deferred to it without thinking.

In a malpractice case, that silence belongs to the plaintiff. If the defense cannot demonstrate the physician was the decision-maker, the narrative is written: the AI made the call, the doctor signed off, the patient was harmed.

That is a losing defense. And right now, most accounts in your book are building exactly that exposure, one undocumented AI encounter at a time.
